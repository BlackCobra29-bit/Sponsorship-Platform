Our Beginning:

Hidrina was established in 2021 by Tigrayans from all over the world. The organizations aims to create a long term service to the families of those who are fighting for our future and sacrificing their lives. It focuses in assisting families of martyrs by utilising variety of resources to create job opportunities to help the families left behind. Our heroes are sacrificing their precious lives for the liberty of Tigray and Tigrayans and as our name indicates we will honour the sacrifice they paid for peace and prosperity of Tigray and Tegaru.

Our Value:

To honour and value the lives paid for the freedom and liberty of our people, we vow to keep our promises to the martyrs and their families.

Our Mission:

Hidrina believes adequate support wasn't provided for our veterans and martyrs families in the past, and we want to change that. we will support our martyrs and their families to provide physical, psychological, Financial and social support services to improve the wellbeing.
