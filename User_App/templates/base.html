About Us

Hidrina was established in 2021 by Tigryans from all over the world. The organisations aims to create a long term service to the families of those who are fighting for our future and sacrificing their lives.

Our Value

To honour and value the lives paid for the freedom and liberty of our people, we vow to keep our promises to the martyrs and their families.
